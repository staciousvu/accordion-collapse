02 What materials are used for these chairs?

Our chairs are constructed using a variety of high-quality materials to ensure comfort, durability, and style. Each material is selected to meet our rigorous standards for performance and aesthetics.

  • The frame is made from premium hardwood or metal, providing stability and strength.
  • Upholstery options include durable fabrics and genuine leather, chosen for their comfort and longevity.
  • Padding and cushioning materials are selected for their resilience and comfort.
  • Eco-friendly finishes are applied to enhance appearance and environmental responsibility.
03 How can I care for these chairs?

Proper care and maintenance of your chairs will extend their lifespan and keep them looking new. Follow these guidelines to ensure the longevity of your furniture.

  • Clean the chairs regularly with a soft, damp cloth to remove dust and dirt.
  • Avoid placing the chairs in direct sunlight to prevent fading and discoloration.
  • Use appropriate cleaning products for the upholstery to maintain its appearance.
  • Periodically check and tighten any loose screws or joints to ensure stability.
